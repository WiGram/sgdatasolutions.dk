About Me

Bridging the gap between data and business value

My Journey

My journey started with an education in mathematical finance and economics and a job in risk management at the Danish Central Bank. Since then, I have gone from data analytics and dashboarding through to building datamarts and now to building data platforms.

I have partnered with organizations across finance, public sector, and consulting - both as an in-house specialist and trusted advisor — to optimize data infrastructure and derive meaningful insights. My approach combines technical expertise with business acumen to deliver practical, scalable solutions.

My Approach

My approach starts with people and purpose. Building something remarkable means understanding business objectives, team dynamics, and end-user needs. I strive to nurture a culture of collaboration, engagement, and empowerment, always contributing to an environment where every team member can thrive and succeed together.

Every project begins with understanding the unique challenges and goals at hand. Through collaborative development and well-documented processes, I commit to delivering a solution designed for smooth integration into your existing team and processes.
