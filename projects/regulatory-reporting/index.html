Regulatory Reporting Solution

Modernizing financial reporting for a major Nordic institution

Overview

Migration of regulatory reporting from a legacy mainframe system to a modern Enterprise Data Warehouse solution for a major Nordic financial services data center. The project focused on maintaining compliance while modernizing the technical infrastructure.

Technical Details

  • Built specialized data mart on EDW platform
  • Implemented reconciliation with existing financial reporting systems
  • Developed user-friendly mapping interface for non-technical staff
  • Created data models aligned with regulatory requirements across Nordic countries, EU, and US

Results & Impact

  • Successfully migrated from mainframe to modern EDW solution
  • Enabled non-technical staff to manage reporting mappings
  • Achieved compliance with multiple regulatory frameworks
  • Established reconciliation with existing financial systems
